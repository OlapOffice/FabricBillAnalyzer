What's New: Combined Sorted Report Feature ENHANCED

Intelligent Sorting

The new Combined Sorted Report organizes your billing data with a specific sorting algorithm:

  • MeterCategory (Ascending) - Groups similar services together
  • ConsumedService (Ascending) - Sub-sorts within categories
  • Cost (Descending) - Shows highest costs first within each group
Export Formats
  • BillSort.csv - Direct CSV export in sorted format
  • Excel Sheet - Included as "Combined_Sorted" tab in Excel reports
